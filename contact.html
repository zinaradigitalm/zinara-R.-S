Questions & Answers

Honest Answers to Common Questions

How soon can I see digital marketing results?
With our Quick Wins audit, you get actionable improvements within 7 days. Most clients see measurable lead growth in 30–60 days. Sustainable, compounding growth is clear after 90 days of consistent strategy execution. We don't promise overnight miracles — we build systems that last.
What's the minimum budget I need for digital marketing?
Our Starter management plan begins at KSh 30,000 per month. Ad spend (for Facebook or Google Ads) is billed separately — we recommend at least KSh 10,000–15,000/month in ad spend for meaningful campaign results. We'll tell you exactly how to allocate your budget from the start.
Do you offer any guarantee?
Yes. We offer a 30-day improvement guarantee. If we don't deliver measurable improvement within the first 30 days, we continue working at no additional cost until we do. We're confident in our process — and we back it up.
Do you work with real estate businesses?
Absolutely. Real estate is one of our strongest verticals. We run property listing campaigns on Facebook and Instagram, build real estate websites optimized for enquiries, manage social pages, and run targeted ads to attract serious property buyers and investors. If you're selling land or houses in Kenya, we know exactly how to reach your buyers.
Who will manage my account?
You get a dedicated senior strategist with direct WhatsApp access. No junior handoffs, no rotating account managers. Your strategist understands your business and is accountable for your results. You'll also get regular check-ins and monthly reports you can actually understand.
Do you work with startups and early-stage businesses?
Yes — startups are our sweet spot. We understand limited budgets and the pressure to show results fast. Our Starter plan is built for businesses in early stages, and we can structure flexible arrangements for businesses with real potential but tight cash flow.
I'm already running ads — can you help?
We'll audit your existing campaigns and identify exactly what's leaking. Most clients who come to us with existing campaigns see 30–50% performance improvement within the first month — just by fixing structural issues and refining targeting. We can take over full management or provide strategic oversight for your team.
